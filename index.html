My Game

Tap Tap Destroy

A dynamic top-down 2D action experience with procedurally generated islands! In this game, no two runs are ever the same. Every level offers a fresh challenge: New island. New enemies. Pure chaos. Master the Controls Success is just a touch away. The controls are intuitive but the gameplay is intense: Move: Drag your finger to navigate the shifting terrain. Destroy: Tap enemies to eliminate them instantly. Strategize: Demolish buildings to cut off enemy buffs and reinforcements. Survive the Mayhem Choose your targets wisely and keep moving. But stay sharp—in this world, the enemies don't just target a character... they attack your finger!
